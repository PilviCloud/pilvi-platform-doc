Themes

Pilvi Platform provides default themes that you can configure with variables to match with your Brand.
The default themes allow you to set your own logo, change colors and modify the look with some additional variables. For more detailed theme adjustments you can also alter the theme with CSS or SCSS.

Site

We currently provide a single default theme that you can alter to your liking with variables.

Product Cards

We provide 10 different themes for you to choose from, and alter your liking with variables.

Messages

We currently provide a single default theme that you can alter to your liking with variables.
